ABOUT OUR WATCHES.

First and foremost, Timex watches are known for their quality and reliability. These watches are built to last and are often passed down from generation to generation. Additionally, Timex watches are a great way to make a statement.

A quality watch is at least made of Stainless Steel. Also, getting skin irritation is rare with an stainless steel watch. Furthermore, a PVD coating is important to ensure that a gold, rosegold, silver or black watch reserves its color. A watch without coating may discolor within a year by frequent use.

Your dad probably had a Timex (and his dad, too) but the brand has come a long way since the days of “It Takes a Licking and Keeps on Ticking.” Now, with inspiration from their extensive archives and creative input from the likes of Todd Snyder and Nigel Cabourn, Timex’s lineup has never been more stylish and diverse. And just as back in the day, they’re still built like tanks.
